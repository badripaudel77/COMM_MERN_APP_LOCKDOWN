Techs : Node.js , Express JS, MongoDB [mongoose]

### Features ###
(i) User can register and login 
(ii) User can perform CRUD operations related to his/her account
(iii) Can create post / and other CRUD operations related to his / her own post
(iv) Can follow and unfollow other users
(v)  Authentication and Authorization with jsonwebtoken[JWT]
(vi) Like posts / unline posts
(vii) User (one) to Post (many) relationships
